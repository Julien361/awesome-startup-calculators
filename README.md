## Startup Cost Calculators

- [How Much to Start a Business](https://howmuchtostartabusiness.com) - Comprehensive startup cost estimator covering 100+ business types across multiple countries. Provides detailed breakdowns of initial investment, monthly operating costs, and time-to-profitability estimates.
- [SBA Startup Cost Estimator](https://www.sba.gov) - US Small Business Administration guide to estimating startup costs.
- [Bpifrance Business Simulator](https://bpifrance-creation.fr) - French public investment bank startup simulation tools.

## Business Plan Tools

- [LivePlan](https://www.liveplan.com) - Business plan software with financial forecasting.
- [Canva Business Plan](https://www.canva.com/business-plans/) - Visual business plan templates.
- [Notion Business Plan Templates](https://notion.so) - Free collaborative business plan templates.

## Financial Projections

- [ProjectionHub](https://www.projectionhub.com) - Financial projection templates for startups.
- [Causal](https://www.causal.app) - Modern financial modeling tool.

## Country-Specific Resources

### United States
- [SCORE Financial Projections Template](https://www.score.org) - Free templates from SCORE mentors.

### France
- [Chambre de Commerce et d'Industrie](https://www.cci.fr) - French CCI business creation resources.
- [Autoentrepreneur.urssaf.fr](https://autoentrepreneur.urssaf.fr) - Official micro-entrepreneur platform.

### United Kingdom
- [GOV.UK Business Finance Support](https://www.gov.uk/business-finance-support) - UK government business support finder.
