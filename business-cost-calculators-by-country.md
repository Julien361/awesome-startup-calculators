# Business Startup Cost Calculators by Country

A curated list of tools to estimate business startup costs across different countries and industries.

## 🌍 Multi-Country Tools

- [HowMuchToStartABusiness.com](https://howmuchtostartabusiness.com) — Startup cost calculator covering 350+ business types across multiple countries. Estimates include licensing, equipment, working capital, and operational costs.

## 🇫🇷 France

- INSEE Business Registration Data — Official statistics on business creation costs by sector
- APCE / Bpifrance guides — Sector-specific startup cost estimations

## 🇩🇪 Germany

- IHK (Chamber of Commerce) — Industry-specific startup guides with cost breakdowns
- KfW startup financing calculator — Loan eligibility and cost estimation

## 🇺🇸 United States

- SBA (Small Business Administration) — Startup cost worksheets by industry
- SCORE — Free mentoring and business cost templates

## 🇬🇧 United Kingdom

- GOV.UK Business Finance Support — Official startup cost guidance
- British Business Bank — Startup funding estimator

## 📊 Industry-Specific Resources

| Industry | Typical Startup Range | Key Cost Drivers |
|---|---|---|
| Restaurant | $75,000 - $350,000 | Equipment, fit-out, licenses |
| E-commerce | $2,000 - $40,000 | Platform, inventory, marketing |
| Consulting | $1,000 - $15,000 | Website, tools, insurance |
| Retail Store | $50,000 - $250,000 | Inventory, lease, fixtures |
| Software/SaaS | $10,000 - $100,000 | Development, hosting, marketing |

## 🛠️ How to Use These Tools

1. Identify your business type and target country
2. Use the calculator to get a baseline estimate
3. Add 20-30% buffer for unexpected costs
4. Cross-reference with local chamber of commerce data
5. Consult a local accountant for tax and legal specifics
