# French Legal Calculators for Entrepreneurs and HR Professionals

A curated list of specialized calculators covering French labor law, taxation, and business compliance. Useful for startups hiring in France.

## Labor Law & HR

- **[Indemnite-Licenciement.fr](https://indemnite-licenciement.fr)** — Calculates statutory severance pay (indemnité légale de licenciement) per the Macron ordinance formula. Supports all seniority levels, collective agreements (conventions collectives), and the 2026 SMIC-indexed rates. Exports PDF for prud'homal proceedings.

- **[Index-Egalite-Pro.fr](https://index-egalite-pro.fr)** — Simulates the gender pay gap index (Index Égalité Femmes-Hommes) required for French companies with 50+ employees. Covers all 5 indicators, identifies gaps before official egapro.travail.gouv.fr submission (deadline: March 1 each year).

## Tax & Payroll

- **[Belasting-Berekenen.be](https://belasting-berekenen.be)** — Belgian and French cross-border tax calculator. Useful for founders with Franco-Belgian operations: compares corporate tax rates, VAT rules, and payroll charges between the two systems.

## Energy & Real Estate Compliance

- **[SRAT-Energies.fr](https://srat-energies.fr)** — Calculates MaPrimeRénov' eligibility and estimated subsidy amounts for French real estate investments. Essential for startup founders investing in commercial or residential property renovation.

## Workforce Training

- **[Trouver-Formation.fr](https://www.trouver-formation.fr)** — Directory of Qualiopi-certified training organizations in France. Helps startups identify eligible CPF-funded training for employees, with filtering by certification type and region.
